Our Story

The Daily Wellness was born in 2018 in Austin, Texas out of a desire to help others live their best and must fulfilled life in their health and wellness. We strive to provide as many people as possible with the best and and most up to date, scientifically-backed information from all things nutrition, fitness, and mental health. We look forward to beginning this journey with you - wherever you are!
